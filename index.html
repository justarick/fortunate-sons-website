Who are we?

Obvious to say, we are the Fortunate Sons: a bunch of misfits, outsiders, nerds and rogues but these are just words, aren't they?

One could say that we also are highly trained in certain fields and therefore absolute professionals for almost everthing.

If there is anything that you need help with - let it be your math homework, your cat stuck in a tree, a guy owing you a few hundred bugs for several years or that castle that has been occupied by a dragon and your family wants to get back: Don't hesitate to contact us.

Some might call us mercenaries - which might be true to a specific point - but there is one thing that makes us different: We are one hundred percent loyal to our customers - if they are as well.

Fortes fortuna adiuvat

"Fortune favors the bold" is not just a slogan but a way of life for us.

Don't get us wrong - we're bold, not stupid. Every mission is planned precisely and well executed. There is no messing around here - even it's a rubber duck you lost.

It is not just us but you as well who is bold and brave for searching for help. We know that this can be hard sometimes but there is nothing to be ashamed for.
